  • Leads pre-qualification

Can Voicebot replace a real consultant?

Voicebot is great at automating repetitive tasks, e.g. pre-qualification interviews during mass hiring or pre-qualification of requests in customer support. It can also automate CRM/HRM system management and reporting. All that saves time and prevents real consultants from burning out. They can concentrate on tasks requiring creativity and higher qualification, which in it’s turn increases performance of your team.
